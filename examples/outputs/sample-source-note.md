# Sample Source Note

These files are demonstration samples, not a complete literature review and
not a submission-ready evidence package.

## What is real

- The sample topic is realistic: RAG-based academic assistant workflows.
- Some seed-source names point to well-known RAG literature categories.

## What still requires verification

- Paper metadata must be checked against the latest official paper version.
- Preprint status, venue status, and citation format must be rechecked before use.
- Rows marked `needs_recheck`, `missing_source`, or `unknown` must not be copied into final prose as verified evidence.
- Local thesis rules require user-provided school templates, advisor notes, rubrics, or official documents.

## Why this sample exists

The sample shows how the skill should stop, qualify, or block prose when the
evidence trail is incomplete.
